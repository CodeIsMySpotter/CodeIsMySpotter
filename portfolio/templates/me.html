Who is behind the code?

~/poznan/put/student

I'm a Computer Science student at Poznan University of Technology, focusing on building reliable, high-performance web systems and exploring backend architecture.

My engineering philosophy revolves around strict static typing, system architecture, predictable data flow, and total determinism. I enjoy diving deep into how systems handle data efficiently, which naturally led me to appreciate compiled languages like Rust and Go. For web interfaces, I prefer clean, lightweight tools like HTMX that allow me to focus heavily on robust server-side logic.
